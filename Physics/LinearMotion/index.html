5. Derivations

Constant $a$. Expand each card for steps.

$v = u + at$

$a = \dfrac{dv}{dt}$ → integrate: $\displaystyle\int_u^v dv = \int_0^t a\,dt$

$$ v - u = at \quad \Rightarrow \quad \boxed{v = u + at} $$

$s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$

Integrate $v = u + at$: $\displaystyle s = \int_0^t (u + at)\,dt = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$

Or use average velocity $\bar{v} = \frac{u+v}{2}$ with $s = \bar{v}\,t$.

$v^2 = u^2 + 2as$

Eliminate $t$ from $v = u + at$ and $s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$ to get $\boxed{v^2 = u^2 + 2as}$.

Range $R = \dfrac{u^2\sin 2\theta}{g}$

$T = \dfrac{2u\sin\theta}{g}$,   $R = v_x T = (u\cos\theta)T$, use $2\sin\theta\cos\theta = \sin 2\theta$.
